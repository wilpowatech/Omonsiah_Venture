About Us

Trusted Medical Equipment Suppliers to Primary, Secondary, and Tertiary Healthcare Centres Across Nigeria

Omonsiah Continental Venture is a reputable medical equipment supply company based at No. 96, 1st East Circular Road, Benin City, Edo State. We specialize in sourcing and delivering high-quality diagnostic tools, surgical instruments, laboratory equipment, hospital beds, and protective medical supplies to both public and private health institutions nationwide.

With a deep understanding of the healthcare landscape in Nigeria, we’ve built a track record of reliability and professionalism through successful partnerships with renowned institutions such as the University of Benin Teaching Hospital (UBTH), Irowa Medicals, and several private clinics and health centers across Edo State and beyond.

At Omonsiah Continental Venture, our commitment is to deliver durable and approved medical solutions that enhance patient care and support healthcare professionals in achieving the best outcomes.
